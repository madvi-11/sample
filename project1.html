Birth of Internet

The Internet was developed in the United States by the "United States Advanced Research Projects Agency" (DARPA). The Internet was first connected in October of 1969 and was called ARPANET. Internet has made it possible to connect with people across the world. Internet is referred to as "network of networks". The Internet is at once a world-wide broadcasting capability, a mechanism for information dissemination, and a medium for collaboration and interaction between individuals and their computers without regard for geographic location.
